What is Tailwind?

Tailwind CSS isn't just another styling framework, its a utility first ideology that makes building modern responsive websites faster and easier. Unlike bootstrap, which comes with predefined components and styles, Tailwind gives you complete control through small reusable utility classes.

What are the installation steps?

Open the terminal(Ctrl + `) in your VS Code. Now, you need to go through tailwindcss.com website, go to -> Docs -> Installation. You can find the installation codes and other required lines to paste in terminal. Follow on with the steps given there and you're ready to go.
